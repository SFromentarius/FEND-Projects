Snail

Did you know? Snails are hermaphroditic!

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Mollusca
  • Class: Gastropoda
  • National Escargot Day: May 24th

Snails are shelled gastropods. They are really cute and slow, they leave a slime trail behind them. It is said that the French consume 40,000 metric tons of snails each year. Yummy!
